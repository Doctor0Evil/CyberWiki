# Code of conduct

CyberWiki contributors are expected to behave professionally and respectfully in all project spaces. Discussions, issues, and reviews should remain focused on technical accuracy, documentation quality, and adherence to the project’s non-fictional scope.

Harassment, discrimination, and persistent off-topic or speculative discussion are not permitted. Project maintainers may moderate interactions, including editing or removing content and restricting participation, to preserve a productive environment centered on rigorous, real-world documentation.
