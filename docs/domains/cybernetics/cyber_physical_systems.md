# Cyber-physical systems

Cyber-physical systems in CyberWiki are documented as integrated compositions of computation, communication, and physical processes with tightly coupled feedback between digital and physical components.[web:88][web:90][web:93] Entries describe system architectures, component roles, communication patterns, and safety or security properties in domains such as medical devices, industrial automation, transportation, and critical infrastructure.[web:88][web:90][web:93]

Each documented system model specifies sensors, actuators, control algorithms, network technologies, and environmental assumptions, along with references to standards, guidance, or research that define expected behavior. Emphasis is placed on reliability, safety, resilience against cyber and physical attacks, and lifecycle considerations such as verification, validation, and evolution over time.[web:86][web:90][web:93]
