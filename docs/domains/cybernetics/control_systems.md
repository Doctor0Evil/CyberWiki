# Control systems

The control systems section documents closed-loop and open-loop architectures used in cyber-physical and human–machine systems. Topics include controller design, stability analysis, feedback modalities, and the mapping between measurable variables and control actions in prosthetics, robotics, and assistive interfaces.[web:3][web:12][web:14]

CyberWiki emphasizes mathematically grounded descriptions, specifying transfer functions, state-space models, or discrete control laws where appropriate to capture real-world behavior.
