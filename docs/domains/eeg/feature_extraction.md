# EEG feature extraction

EEG feature extraction in CyberWiki focuses on reproducible methods that map raw electroencephalographic time series into quantitative descriptors used for classification, decoding, and monitoring tasks.[web:66][web:67][web:70] Documented techniques include temporal-domain measures such as Hjorth parameters, frequency-domain measures such as power spectral density over canonical bands, and time–frequency decompositions such as wavelet-based energy distributions.[web:66][web:70]

Entries in this section characterize each method by its mathematical definition, parameterization, computational requirements, and typical application contexts in clinical, research, or BCI systems. Where relevant, CyberWiki links feature extraction methods to specific pipelines, toolboxes, or standards that define their reference implementations.[web:66][web:67][web:73]
