# Open EEG and electrophysiology datasets

CyberWiki catalogs openly available EEG and related electrophysiology datasets that can be used to evaluate acquisition pipelines, feature extraction methods, and BCI models.[web:101] Entries include corpus names, modalities, subject counts, recording conditions, licensing terms, and links to official download locations or registries.[web:101]

For each dataset, CyberWiki notes adherence to data standards such as BIDS or NWB where applicable and highlights any accompanying documentation that constrains usage or interpretation. This information supports reproducible research and informed selection of datasets for specific technical questions.[web:101]
