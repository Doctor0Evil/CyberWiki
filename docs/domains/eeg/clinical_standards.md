# EEG clinical standards

The EEG clinical standards section catalogs guidelines, standards, and reference practices that govern how EEG is used in diagnostic and monitoring contexts.[web:66][web:70] CyberWiki describes the structure and intent of major standards, referencing published documents that define electrode placement schemes, recording protocols, and reporting conventions.[web:66][web:70]

By linking these standards to acquisition pipelines, device models, and feature extraction methods, CyberWiki provides a coherent picture of how clinical EEG practice constrains system design and data interpretation. This helps practitioners maintain alignment with recognized medical and regulatory expectations.[web:66][web:70]
