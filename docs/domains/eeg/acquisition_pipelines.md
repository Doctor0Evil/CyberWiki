# EEG acquisition pipelines

EEG acquisition pipelines describe the ordered stages from electrode placement and amplification through digitization, filtering, artifact handling, and storage. CyberWiki documents pipelines used in clinical diagnostics, research experiments, and BCI applications, with attention to reproducibility and regulatory expectations.[web:12][web:14]

Entries specify hardware parameters, sampling regimes, filter characteristics, and data formats, forming a basis for consistent interpretation and comparison across deployments.
