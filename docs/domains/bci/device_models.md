# BCI device models

BCI device models in CyberWiki describe concrete hardware and software configurations used to acquire, interpret, or stimulate neural activity. Entries cover signal acquisition interfaces, implantable arrays, non-invasive sensor arrangements, and associated safety and calibration procedures.[web:12][web:14]

Each model is linked to provenance records referencing manufacturer documentation, regulatory submissions, or peer-reviewed studies, allowing users to trace claims about capabilities, limitations, and deployment contexts to primary sources.
