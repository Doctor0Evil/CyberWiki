# Governance for BCI safety

Governance for BCI safety in CyberWiki addresses how organizations manage policies, procedures, and documentation to maintain safe operation of brain–computer interface technologies over their lifecycle.[web:90][web:95] Entries outline roles and responsibilities, change control requirements, incident reporting structures, and audit mechanisms aligned with broader quality and accreditation frameworks.[web:90][web:95]

By linking governance artifacts to specific device models, signal processing pipelines, and clinical standards, CyberWiki supports traceable reasoning about how safety requirements are propagated into technical and operational controls. This enables integrated views across technical design, laboratory practice, and regulatory expectations.[web:90][web:95]
