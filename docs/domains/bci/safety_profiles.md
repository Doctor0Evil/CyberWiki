# BCI safety profiles

BCI safety profiles in CyberWiki describe risk considerations and mitigation strategies associated with brain–computer interfaces in clinical, research, and assistive contexts.[web:66][web:90][web:95] Documentation covers aspects such as tissue compatibility for invasive devices, exposure limits for stimulation protocols, electromagnetic safety for non-invasive systems, and software or data integrity risks in decoding pipelines.[web:66][web:90]

Each safety profile links specific hazards to controls, monitoring mechanisms, and validation or assurance activities. References to standards, regulatory guidance, and laboratory quality frameworks are included where they constrain design, testing, or operation of BCI systems.[web:90][web:95]
