# Regulatory landscape overview

The regulatory landscape overview in CyberWiki provides a high-level map of the standards, regulations, and guidance documents relevant to cybernetics, BCI, EEG, and nanoswarm systems across medical, industrial, and other application domains.[web:71][web:74][web:90] It identifies key authorities, harmonized standards, and cross-cutting requirements related to safety, cybersecurity, data protection, and quality management.[web:71][web:90][web:93]

This overview is designed as a starting point for deeper exploration through linked compliance profiles, safety documents, and domain-specific standards pages. It emphasizes verifiable references and avoids speculative forecasting about future regulation, focusing instead on current, publicly documented expectations.[web:71][web:74][web:90]
