# Nanoswarm regulatory considerations

Nanoswarm regulatory considerations focus on how nanoscale and microscale swarms intersect with safety, ethics, and compliance frameworks in sectors such as medicine, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation.[web:71][web:74] CyberWiki summarizes applicable guidance from standards bodies, regulators, and professional organizations, concentrating on requirements for safety testing, risk assessment, and lifecycle management.[web:71][web:74]

Entries in this section highlight obligations related to documentation, cybersecurity, traceability, and decommissioning of nanoswarm deployments, helping integrators align technical designs with current regulatory expectations. Where zero trust or advanced cybersecurity architectures are relevant, references to corresponding guidelines are included.[web:71][web:74]
