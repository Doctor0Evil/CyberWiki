# Swarm topologies

Swarm topologies characterize how nanoscale or microscale agents are organized and interconnected for coordinated behavior. CyberWiki documents lattice, cluster, and dynamic graph arrangements along with the communication and control mechanisms that sustain stable operation under realistic physical constraints.[web:12][web:14]

Descriptions focus on experimentally supported designs and simulations that align with current literature, avoiding unsupported extrapolations about speculative capabilities.
