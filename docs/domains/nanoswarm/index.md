# Nanoswarm systems

Nanoswarm documentation in CyberWiki addresses coordinated collections of engineered nanoscale or microscale units used for sensing, actuation, or targeted delivery. Coverage is limited to systems described in peer-reviewed literature, patents, or regulatory pathways.[web:12][web:14]

The focus is on swarm topology, communication protocols, control strategies, and safety or regulatory considerations relevant to deployment in medical, industrial, or environmental contexts.
